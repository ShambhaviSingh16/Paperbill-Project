Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about PaperBill for automobile businesses

Is PaperBill suitable for my auto parts store?

Yes, PaperBill is specifically designed for automobile businesses including auto parts stores, service centers, and dealerships. Our software includes specialized features like parts inventory management, vehicle-specific invoicing, and service job tracking that are tailored to your industry.

How does the inventory management work?

PaperBill's inventory system tracks all your parts and accessories with real-time updates. You can set minimum stock levels, track items by barcode or batch number, manage suppliers, and receive automatic alerts when stock is low. The system automatically updates inventory levels when you create invoices or receive new stock.

Can I access PaperBill on my mobile device?

Absolutely! PaperBill is fully responsive and works on all devices including smartphones and tablets. We also offer dedicated mobile apps for iOS and Android that allow you to create invoices, check inventory, and manage your business from anywhere.

What kind of support do you offer?

We provide comprehensive support including email, phone, and live chat during business hours. All customers also get access to our detailed knowledge base, video tutorials, and onboarding sessions to help you get started. Enterprise customers receive a dedicated account manager for personalized support.

How secure is my business data?

Data security is our top priority. PaperBill uses bank-grade 256-bit encryption for all data transmission and storage. We perform regular backups and offer two-factor authentication for added security. Your data is stored on secure servers with 99.9% uptime guarantee.

Can I customize invoices with my shop's branding?

Yes, PaperBill allows full customization of your invoices. You can add your logo, change colors to match your brand, modify fields to include specific information your customers need, and even create multiple templates for different types of transactions.

Does PaperBill integrate with accounting software?

PaperBill integrates seamlessly with popular accounting software including Tally, QuickBooks, and others. You can export your financial data with a single click, eliminating the need for manual data entry and reducing errors in your bookkeeping.
