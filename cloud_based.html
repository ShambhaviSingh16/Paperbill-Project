Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about PaperBill Cloud

Is my data safe in the cloud?

Yes, your data is protected with enterprise-grade security measures including 256-bit SSL encryption, regular backups, and two-factor authentication. We use secure data centers with 99.9% uptime guarantees.

What happens if I lose internet connection?

PaperBill Cloud has an offline mode that allows you to continue working without internet. All your changes will automatically sync once your connection is restored.

Can I access PaperBill Cloud on mobile?

Absolutely! PaperBill Cloud is fully responsive and works on all devices including smartphones and tablets. We also offer dedicated mobile apps for iOS and Android.

How often is my data backed up?

Your data is backed up automatically every hour, with additional daily and weekly backups stored securely in multiple locations.

Can I export my data if I want to switch?

Yes, you can export all your data at any time in standard formats (Excel, CSV, PDF) for easy migration to other systems.

How many users can access my account?

You can add unlimited users to your account with different permission levels to control what each user can access and modify.
