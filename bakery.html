Absolutely! PaperBill includes specialized recipe management tools that let you track ingredients, calculate product costs, and manage your bakery recipes all in one place.

You can enter expiry dates for perishable items when adding them to inventory. PaperBill will alert you when products are nearing expiration so you can use them or offer discounts.

Yes, PaperBill supports label printing with customizable templates that can include product names, ingredients, prices, barcodes, and expiry dates.
